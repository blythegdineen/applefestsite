Details

Timing

In 2018, the festival will be occuring from September 28th to 30th in the heart of Downtown Ithaca. Vendors will be there from 10am to 5pm.

Transportation

Parking is limited in the Ithaca Commons so we suggest using the Tomkins Consolidated Area Transit (TCAT) and getting off at the Commons. Please check the TCAT Website to find details on your ideal route. Additionally, the Ithaca Commons is about a 15 minute walk from Collegetown, which in turn is about a 20 minute walk from North Campus.

A Note to Cornell Students

Just a reminder to Cornell students that if you are in your first year, the TCAT is free, otherwise the TCAT is free after 6:00pm and all day on weekends. Take advantage of that resource to take a trip to our festival!
